About

Eazy Crypto is a proprietary market-making firm.
We add value to the markets in which we participate, by providing liquidity in a full range of financial instruments across multiple asset classes.
We design and build cutting-edge technology and serve as a leader in the global derivatives marketplace.
We employ an innovative trading strategy that integrates technology with a quantitative automated market-making approach.
We respond quickly to constantly evolving markets, because our team members – traders, software engineers and quants – work side-by-side in an open, collaborative environment. This unique approach enables us to successfully leverage our strategies across a multitude of products and global exchanges.
Our success begins with talented individuals – and is maintained and driven by a unified team effort.


Trading

We currently have nine trading teams, each focused on separate asset classes.
Our trading teams collectively implement our trading strategies, while monitoring the markets to ensure our strategies are effectively reacting to changing market conditions.
in addition, our traders participate in cross-functional teams, comprising traders, software engineers and quants, to develop new and enhance existing trading strategies.
These teams work to solve hard problems by leveraging technology and thinking innovatively.

Technology

Technology is a driving force of our business; we make ongoing capital investments to ensure our employees have the tools they need to excel.
Eazy Crypto uses proprietary software and analytic systems, developed in-house, to provide liquidity and identify trading opportunities.
We credit our technological success to our unique work environment, which eliminates the traditional divide between idea makers and programmers and technology implementers.
This highly collaborative atmosphere allows us to efficiently and effectively integrate technology and trading to achieve success.

Quantitative Research

At Eazy Crypto, quantitative research is the foundation for our trading strategies.
Our quants develop mathematical models, based on historical data, to represent markets and behavioral patterns.
These models serve as road-maps for our pricing strategies, market decisions and risk management.
Our quants are experts in their field: 50% have a master’s degree, 20% have PhDs. 75% have industry experience and 40% have 25+ years of experience.

Our Guiding Principles are the way we believe we should operate as a firm.
